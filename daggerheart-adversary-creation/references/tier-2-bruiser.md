# Tier 2 Bruiser

Use for heavy melee threats that can dominate a lane.

- Safe band: Difficulty 14-16; thresholds 13/24 to 16/28; HP 7-8; Stress 4-5; ATK +1 to +2; damage 2d8+5 to 2d12+4
- Default center: Difficulty 16; thresholds 14/27; HP 7; Stress 5; ATK +2; damage 2d8+5
- Feature emphasis: multi-target hits, charges, direct damage, forced movement
- Exemplars: Minotaur Wrecker, Giant Hammerer, Shark
