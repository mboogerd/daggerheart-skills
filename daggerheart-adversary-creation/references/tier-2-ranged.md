# Tier 2 Ranged

Use for accurate, high-damage enemies the PCs must close with or outmaneuver.

- Safe band: Difficulty 13-14; thresholds 7/14 to 9/16; HP 4-5; Stress 3-4; ATK +2 to +3; damage 2d8+4 to 2d12+6
- Default center: Difficulty 13; thresholds 7/14; HP 5; Stress 4; ATK +3; damage 2d10+4
- Feature emphasis: cover, snares, vulnerable setup, or long-range punishment
- Exemplars: Icewaste Hunter, War Wizard, Ghost Archer
