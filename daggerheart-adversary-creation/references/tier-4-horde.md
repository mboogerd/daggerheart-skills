# Tier 4 Horde

Use for terrifying groups that are still legible as one unit.

- Safe band: Difficulty 17-19; thresholds 20/34 to 25/45; HP 6-7; Stress 3-4; ATK +1 to +2; damage 4d8+10 to 4d10+12, dropping by roughly half
- Default center: Difficulty 18; thresholds 22/38; HP 7; Stress 4; ATK +2; damage 4d10+12, dropping to 4d6+6
- Feature emphasis: `Horde (X)` plus one scene-pressure or retaliation hook
- Exemplars: High-tier undead pack
