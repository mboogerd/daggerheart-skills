# Tier 2 Horde

Use for dangerous groups that open hard and weaken visibly.

- Safe band: Difficulty 11-13; thresholds 9/16 to 12/20; HP 5-6; Stress 3; ATK -1 to +1; damage 2d8+4 to 2d10+4, dropping to about half
- Default center: Difficulty 12; thresholds 10/18; HP 6; Stress 3; ATK +0; damage 2d10+4, dropping to 2d6+2
- Feature emphasis: `Horde (X)` plus one swarming or pressure-reaction feature
- Exemplars: Archer Formation, Hellhound Pack, Shock Eel Swarm
