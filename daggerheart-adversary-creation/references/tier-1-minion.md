# Tier 1 Minion

Use for fragile enemies that scale the scene without slowing it down.

- Safe band: Difficulty 8-11; thresholds None; HP 1; Stress 1; ATK -4 to -1; fixed damage 1-2; `Minion (3)` to `Minion (5)`
- Default center: Difficulty 10; thresholds None; HP 1; Stress 1; ATK -3; damage 1; `Minion (3)`
- Feature emphasis: usually only `Minion (X)` and `Group Attack`
- Exemplars: Giant Rat, Rotted Zombie, Jagged Knife Lackey
