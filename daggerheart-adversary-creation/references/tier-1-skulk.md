# Tier 1 Skulk

Use for ambushers, sneaks, and hit-and-run threats.

- Safe band: Difficulty 11-12; thresholds 4/8 to 5/10; HP 3-4; Stress 2-3; ATK +1 to +2; damage 1d4+4 to 1d6+3
- Default center: Difficulty 12; thresholds 4/8; HP 3; Stress 3; ATK +1; damage 1d4+4
- Feature emphasis: Hidden, advantage, repositioning, backstab, or isolation pressure
- Exemplars: Jagged Knife Shadow, Dire Wolf, Small Flame Ooze
