# Tier 4 Social

Use for god-touched authorities, immortal judges, and socially overwhelming powers.

- Safe band: Difficulty 18-20; thresholds 14/24 to 18/30; HP 4-5; Stress 6-8; ATK -1 to +1; damage 4d4+4 to 4d6+4
- Default center: Difficulty 19; thresholds 16/26; HP 4; Stress 7; ATK +0; damage 4d6+4
- Feature emphasis: command of institutions, terror, divine authority, scene-wide noncombat pressure
- Exemplars: extrapolate from lower-tier courtiers and rulers, but raise the scale of leverage
