# Tier 4 Bruiser

Use for catastrophic melee threats and elite juggernauts.

- Safe band: Difficulty 17-19; thresholds 25/45 to 34/55; HP 9-11; Stress 4-6; ATK +3 to +4; damage 4d8+10 to 4d10+12
- Default center: Difficulty 18; thresholds 30/50; HP 10; Stress 5; ATK +3; damage 4d10+12
- Feature emphasis: devastating melee, control through force, huge punishment for clustering
- Exemplars: Flux Horror, Empowered Zombie Titan
