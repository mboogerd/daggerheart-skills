# Tier 4 Standard

Use for late-game elite line troops that still exist below boss level.

- Safe band: Difficulty 18-20; thresholds 22/40 to 26/50; HP 5-6; Stress 3-4; ATK +3 to +4; damage 4d8+5 to 4d10+6
- Default center: Difficulty 19; thresholds 22/50; HP 5; Stress 3; ATK +3; damage 4d8+5
- Feature emphasis: one strong identity feature and crisp tactical purpose
- Exemplars: Bladedance Jester
