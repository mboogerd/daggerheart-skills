# Tier 3 Horde

Use for large supernatural groups that remain dangerous even after attrition.

- Safe band: Difficulty 15-16; thresholds 15/25 to 20/32; HP 6-7; Stress 3-4; ATK +0 to +1; damage 3d10+8 to 3d12+10, dropping by roughly half
- Default center: Difficulty 16; thresholds 15/25; HP 6; Stress 3; ATK +1; damage 3d12+10, dropping to 3d6+5
- Feature emphasis: `Horde (X)` plus one appetite, swarm, or retaliation pattern
- Exemplars: Covetous Miners
