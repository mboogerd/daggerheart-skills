# Tier 1 Horde

Use for swarms and packs represented as one unit.

- Safe band: Difficulty 8-11; thresholds 6/10 to 6/12; HP 5-6; Stress 3; ATK -1 to +0; damage 1d8+2 to 1d10+2, dropping to about half at half HP
- Default center: Difficulty 9; thresholds 6/12; HP 6; Stress 3; ATK -1; damage 1d10+2, dropping to 1d4+2
- Feature emphasis: one `Horde (X)` passive and maybe one reactive or situational attack rider
- Exemplars: Zombie Pack, Rat Swarm, Mosquito Cloud
