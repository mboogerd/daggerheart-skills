# Tier 3 Skulk

Use for elite predators and assassins that capitalize on openings.

- Safe band: Difficulty 17-18; thresholds 12/24 to 16/28; HP 5-6; Stress 4-5; ATK +3; damage 3d8+4 to 3d10+4
- Default center: Difficulty 18; thresholds 14/26; HP 6; Stress 4; ATK +3; damage 3d8+4
- Feature emphasis: camouflage, approach denial, punishing isolated targets, repositioning
- Exemplars: Vampire Hound, Shadow Demon, Acid Ooze
