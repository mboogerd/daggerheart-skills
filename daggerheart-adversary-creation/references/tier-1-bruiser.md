# Tier 1 Bruiser

Use for physically overwhelming front-line threats.

- Safe band: Difficulty 10-13; thresholds 7/13 to 9/17; HP 5-7; Stress 2-4; ATK -1 to +2; damage 1d8+3 to 1d12+3
- Default center: Difficulty 12; thresholds 8/15; HP 6; Stress 3; ATK +1; damage 1d10+3
- Feature emphasis: knockback, line-breaking, sweeps, big melee pressure
- Exemplars: Vineback Bully, Skeleton Colossus, Pirate Bruiser
