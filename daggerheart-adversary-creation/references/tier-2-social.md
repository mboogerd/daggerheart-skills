# Tier 2 Social

Use for highly capable schemers and social controllers.

- Safe band: Difficulty 14-15; thresholds 7/14 to 9/18; HP 3-4; Stress 4-5; ATK -3 to -1; damage 2d4+2 to 2d4+3
- Default center: Difficulty 15; thresholds 8/14; HP 3; Stress 5; ATK -2; damage 2d4+2
- Feature emphasis: leverage, favors, pressure, price control, status manipulation
- Exemplars: Courtier, Merchant Magnate, Espionage Agent
