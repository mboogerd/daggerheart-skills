# Tier 1 Social

Use for negotiations, interrogations, and coercive encounters where combat is secondary.

- Safe band: Difficulty 12-14; thresholds 4/8 to 6/None; HP 2-3; Stress 3-4; ATK -4 to -2; damage 1d4+1 to 1d4+2
- Default center: Difficulty 13; thresholds 5/9; HP 3; Stress 4; ATK -3; damage 1d4+2
- Feature emphasis: passive defenses, leverage, stress pressure, social consequences
- Exemplars: Village Elder, Merchant, Petty Noble
