# Tier 1 Support

Use for buffers, debuffers, and condition-focused helpers.

- Safe band: Difficulty 11-12; thresholds 6/10 to 7/11; HP 4-5; Stress 4-5; ATK +0 to +1; damage 1d4+2 to 1d6+2
- Default center: Difficulty 11; thresholds 6/10; HP 4; Stress 5; ATK +0; damage 1d4+2
- Feature emphasis: end conditions, impose Vulnerable or Restrained, support allies, zone control
- Exemplars: Forest Druid, Jagged Knife Hexer
