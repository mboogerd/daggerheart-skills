# Tier 2 Standard

Use for reliable mid-tier rank-and-file enemies.

- Safe band: Difficulty 13-15; thresholds 8/16 to 10/20; HP 5-6; Stress 2-3; ATK +1 to +2; damage 2d6+3 to 2d8+3
- Default center: Difficulty 14; thresholds 9/18; HP 5; Stress 3; ATK +2; damage 2d6+3
- Feature emphasis: one faction-defining feature and one simple tactical rider
- Exemplars: Veteran Guard, Ghost Soldier, Necrotic Experiment
