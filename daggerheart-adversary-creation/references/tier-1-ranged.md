# Tier 1 Ranged

Use for archers, hexers, and other distance threats.

- Safe band: Difficulty 10-12; thresholds 4/7 to 6/10; HP 3-4; Stress 2-3; ATK +1 to +2; damage 1d8+2 to 1d10+2
- Default center: Difficulty 11; thresholds 4/7; HP 3; Stress 2; ATK +1; damage 1d8+2
- Feature emphasis: hard hits from safety, cover use, traps, or anti-approach tools
- Exemplars: Archer Guard, Jagged Knife Sniper
