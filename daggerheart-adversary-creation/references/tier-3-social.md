- Safe band: Difficulty 17-18; thresholds 10/18 to 14/24; HP 3-5; Stress 5-7; ATK -1 to +1; damage 3d4+3 to 3d6+3
- Default center: Difficulty 17; thresholds 12/20; HP 4; Stress 6; ATK +0; damage 3d6+3
- Feature emphasis: social pressure, legal or political leverage, scene-level consequences
- Exemplars: Crowned Sovereign
