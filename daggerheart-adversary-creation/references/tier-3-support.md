# Tier 3 Support

Use for powerful enablers that shape the battlefield without being the primary damage source.

- Safe band: Difficulty 16-18; thresholds 14/24 to 18/30; HP 5-6; Stress 5-6; ATK +2 to +3; damage 3d6+4 to 3d8+4
- Default center: Difficulty 17; thresholds 16/28; HP 5; Stress 6; ATK +3; damage 3d6+4
- Feature emphasis: strong ally protection, conditions, terrain generation, fear-fueled utility
- Exemplars: Greater Water Elemental, Gate Construct, Demon Treasurer
