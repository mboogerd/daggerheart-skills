# Tier 3 Bruiser

Use for large, durable monsters that hit with major force.

- Safe band: Difficulty 15-17; thresholds 20/32 to 26/42; HP 8-9; Stress 4-5; ATK +2 to +3; damage 3d10+5 to 3d12+5
- Default center: Difficulty 16; thresholds 24/38; HP 8; Stress 4; ATK +2; damage 3d12+5
- Feature emphasis: heavy melee, shockwaves, throws, fear-inducing reactions
- Exemplars: Dire Wight, Greater Earth Elemental, Ancient Treant
