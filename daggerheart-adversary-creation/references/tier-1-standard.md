# Tier 1 Standard

Use for rank-and-file foes and the backbone of a faction.

- Safe band: Difficulty 11-13; thresholds 4/8 to 6/10; HP 4-5; Stress 2-3; ATK +0 to +1; damage 1d6+1 to 1d8+1
- Default center: Difficulty 12; thresholds 5/9; HP 5; Stress 2; ATK +1; damage 1d6+1
- Feature emphasis: one identity feature, one simple control or positioning move at most
- Exemplars: Bladed Guard, Jagged Knife Bandit, Glass Snake
