# Tier 2 Minion

Use for tougher swarms that still die in one hit each.

- Safe band: Difficulty 12-14; thresholds None; HP 1; Stress 1; ATK -2 to +0; fixed damage 3-4; `Minion (5)` to `Minion (7)`
- Default center: Difficulty 13; thresholds None; HP 1; Stress 1; ATK -1; damage 3; `Minion (6)`
- Feature emphasis: keep the basic minion package unless the fiction really needs one extra twist
- Exemplars: Pressed Civilian, Cult Minion, Giant Initiate
