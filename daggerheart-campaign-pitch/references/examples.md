# Examples

## Example: Heroic Forest Crisis

- Pitch: An ancient forest deity has been wounded, and the land is responding with violent overgrowth, strange omens, and territorial spirits. The PCs are among the few people with reasons to cross the blighted wilds instead of fleeing them. If they cannot learn who struck the first blow and why, the region will be consumed by a living catastrophe.
- Tone: mythic, urgent, mournful, hopeful
- Themes: stewardship, grief, sacred violence
- Starting pressure: villages are becoming unlivable

## Example: Frontier Monster Campaign

- Pitch: Trade routes across the drylands are failing as colossal creatures awaken beneath the earth and turn safe settlements into panic-stricken strongholds. The PCs are tied to the people, debts, and rivalries of the frontier, so they cannot simply ride past the danger. Every victory buys time, but every battle reveals a deeper cause behind the awakening.
