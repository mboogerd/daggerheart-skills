# Role Pairings

Use these pairings when you want encounter roles to reinforce one another.

## Reliable Pairings

- **Leader + Standards:** the classic disciplined enemy formation.
- **Leader + Ranged:** the leader creates time and position for the ranged units to matter.
- **Bruiser + Support:** the Bruiser occupies attention while Support extends the fight.
- **Bruiser + Ranged:** the Bruiser locks down melee while ranged attackers punish those who stay back.
- **Skulk + Minions:** the Minions create noise and pressure while the Skulk hunts openings.
- **Horde + Environment:** broad pressure becomes much more dangerous when terrain or hazards limit movement.
- **Solo + Environment:** best when the battlefield itself helps the Solo avoid feeling outnumbered.

## Warning Signs

- Too many Supports can make the scene feel fiddly instead of tense.
- Too many Solos or pseudo-Solos can produce grind rather than drama.
- Too many roles that do the same job reduce player decision space.
