# Output Rubric

Grade each output on a 1 to 5 scale.

## Activation Fit

- Did the right skill activate?
- Did it avoid overreaching into a neighboring skill's job?

## Structural Quality

- Did the output match the skill's intended template or output shape?
- Was anything essential omitted?

## Daggerheart Usefulness

- Did the answer reflect Daggerheart-style prep and pressure?
- Did it feel specific enough to use at the table?

## Clarity

- Is the answer easy to scan and act on?
- Are priorities and next steps obvious?

## Follow-On Composition

- Does the output naturally hand off to the next likely skill?
