# Worldbuilding At Session Zero

Session zero worldbuilding should create direction, not exhaustive canon.

## What To Establish

- the starting region or social center
- one to three current problems in the wider world
- a few striking locations, factions, or institutions
- cultural or magical assumptions the players need for character creation
- why the PCs already care about this place or these problems

## Good Prompts

- What danger or instability currently shapes daily life here?
- What place in this region is famous, sacred, feared, or politically important?
- What rumor does everyone know?
- What kind of power structure do ordinary people live under?
- What does this region need from heroes right now?

## Map Rule

Put down only enough locations to generate movement, mystery, and hooks. Leave blanks for later sessions so the setting can keep responding to play.
