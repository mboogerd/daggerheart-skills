# Examples

## Example Opening Questions

- What kind of fantasy story are you most excited to tell right now?
- What tones do you actively want at the table, and what tones should stay rare or absent?
- What kinds of subjects are okay, and what should remain off-screen or excluded?
- What kinds of relationships between PCs sound fun to explore?

## Example End-State

By the end of session zero, the GM should know:

- the campaign premise the table wants
- tone and safety boundaries
- the starting region or frame assumptions
- what makes each PC matter
- what to prep for session one
