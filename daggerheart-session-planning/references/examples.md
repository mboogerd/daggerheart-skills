# Examples

## Example Session Brief

- Purpose: bring the PCs to the occupied border town and show how far the takeover has progressed.
- Beats: arrival, hidden ally contact, failed rescue, street-level clash, cliffhanger at the mayor's house.
- Active threats: invasion countdown, frightened civilians, compromised town guard.
- Exit hook: the occupiers reveal they already expected the PCs.

## Example Session With Downtime

- Purpose: let the party recover after a hard victory while surfacing two new obligations.
- Beats: quiet recovery, difficult conversation, request for aid, discovery of worsening off-screen threat.
