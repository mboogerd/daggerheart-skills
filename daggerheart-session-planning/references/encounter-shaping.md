# Encounter Shaping In Session Prep

Combat in Daggerheart should reveal something about the story, the world, or the characters. A good fight is not just a damage exchange.

## Before You Add Combat

Answer:

- Why is violence happening here?
- What objective matters besides survival?
- What does the opposing side want?
- What changes if the PCs win but fail the objective?

## Tools For Better Encounters

- objectives beyond "defeat everyone"
- countdowns for ritual, collapse, arrival, pursuit, or escape
- environments that change movement or risk
- phased changes in foes or battlefield

## Fear Rule

Keep enough Fear in reserve that you can interrupt momentum and make the scene feel alive when needed.
