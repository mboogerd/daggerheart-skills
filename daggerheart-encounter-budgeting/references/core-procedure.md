# Battle Points

## Base Formula

Starting battle points:

`(3 x number of PCs) + 2`

## Adjustments

- Subtract 1 for a shorter or easier fight.
- Add 2 for a longer or more dangerous fight.
- Subtract 2 if using 2 or more Solos.
- Subtract 2 if all adversaries deal extra damage.
- Add 1 if using lower-tier adversaries.
- Add 1 if the encounter has no Bruiser, Horde, Leader, or Solo.

## Spend Rates

- Minion group equal to party size: 1
- Social or Support: 1
- Horde, Ranged, Skulk, Standard: 2
- Leader: 3
- Bruiser: 4
- Solo: 5

## Rule

Treat the budget as rough balancing help, not a promise of fairness.

## Practical Use

- Start with party size.
- Adjust for desired pacing and danger.
- Spend points to get a first pass.
- Then ask whether the encounter has enough motive, movement, and texture to be memorable.

## Common Patterns

- **Intro fight:** easier budget, one obvious role anchor, little complexity.
- **Patrol or skirmish:** average budget, mix of Standards with one role that changes player priorities.
- **Boss fight:** higher pressure, often a Leader or Solo plus support structure.
- **Attrition scene:** lower lethality but more bodies, movement pressure, or environment pressure.
