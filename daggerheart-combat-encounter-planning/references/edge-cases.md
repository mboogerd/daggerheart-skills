# Edge Cases

## Flat Fights

If the plan is only "four Standards in a room," add a second pressure:

- environment hazard
- timer
- support unit
- ranged threat
- enemy objective beyond killing the PCs

## Overbuilt Fights

If the encounter has too many moving parts, cut first from:

- extra special enemies
- redundant hazards
- overlapping countdowns

Keep one clear backbone and one or two secondary pressures.

## Leader Without Followers

A Leader usually needs allies to matter. If they have none:

- downgrade the plan to a different role
- or add a summon, command, or reinforcement structure

## Solo Without Support

A Solo without extra action economy, reactions, or scene support can become static.

Give them:

- explicit extra spotlight potential
- major area pressure
- or an environment that keeps the scene moving

## Decorative Environment

If the environment does not alter decisions, remove it or turn it into active pressure.

## Premature Downstream Work

If the plan starts generating multiple finished stat blocks, stop and convert them into handoff notes.
