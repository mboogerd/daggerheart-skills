# Examples

## Example 1: Patrol Fight

- Narrative function: introduce a faction's battlefield behavior
- Budget: average
- Backbone: 1 slot with Count 2, Role Standard, Points 4
- Pressure role: 1 slot with Count 1, Role Ranged, Points 2
- Environment: narrow trail with cover and unstable footing
- Escalation: horn call countdown can summon Minions
- Handoffs: adapt-existing Standards, create-unnamed Ranged scout

## Example 2: Boss With Followers

- Narrative function: confrontation with a local commander
- Budget: harder fight
- Backbone: 1 slot with Count 1, Role Leader, Points 3; 1 slot with Count 2, Role Standard, Points 4; 1 slot with Count 1, Role Support, Points 1
- Environment: fortified gatehouse with overlapping sight lines
- Escalation: if the Leader survives three beats, the gate closes and retreat becomes harder
- Handoffs: create-named Leader, lookup-existing-unnamed guards, create-unnamed Support

## Example 3: Environmental Crisis Battle

- Narrative function: stop a ritual while surviving the battlefield
- Budget: average to hard
- Backbone: 1 slot with Count 1, Role Horde, Points 2; 1 slot with Count 1, Role Support, Points 1
- Environment: unstable ritual site is the primary pressure
- Escalation: countdown advances whenever a PC fails with Fear near the focus point
- Handoffs: environment-design first, then lookup or create adversaries that fit the ritual site
