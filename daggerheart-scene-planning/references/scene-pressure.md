# Scene Pressure

Choose one main pressure source:

- Time pressure
- Emotional pressure
- Social pressure
- Physical danger
- Scarcity
- Split priorities

## Use

One strong pressure is usually enough. Add a second only if it creates a better choice, not more noise.

## Pressure Questions

Ask:

- What happens if the PCs wait?
- What must they risk to get what they want?
- What will divide their priorities?
- What information is expensive to obtain?
