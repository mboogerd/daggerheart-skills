# Examples

## Example Social Scene

- Purpose: pressure the PCs to take a side before open conflict starts.
- Opening image: a noble feast continuing in brittle politeness after an assassination attempt.
- Pressure: reputation and divided loyalties.
- Turn: the host publicly asks one PC to condemn an ally.

## Example Exploration Scene

- Purpose: reveal that the ruins are still semi-functional.
- Opening image: quiet halls that hum when domain magic is used nearby.
- Pressure: curiosity versus safety.
- Exit states: clue gained, trap triggered, or a machine awakened.
