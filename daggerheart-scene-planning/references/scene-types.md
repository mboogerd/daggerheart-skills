# Scene Types

Use the dominant scene type to decide what to prep.

## Investigation

Prep secrets, conflicting accounts, clues that point in more than one direction, and at least one cost for learning too much too quickly.

## Social Confrontation

Prep what each NPC wants, what leverage they have, what they fear, and what would force a change in position.

## Exploration

Prep wonder, uncertainty, environmental pressure, and one discovery that changes what the PCs think the place is.

## Action Or Combat

Prep motives, objectives, movement pressure, and the thing that will change if the fight runs long.
