# Examples

## Example Recommendation

- Recommended frame: Motherboard
- Why it fits: the table wants science-fantasy salvage, weird ruins, and a campaign where discovery matters as much as combat.
- GM load: moderate to high because the frame benefits from custom relics, machines, and strange locations.
- Best for PCs who are scavengers, mystics, wanderers, ex-technicians, or people tied to old systems.

## Example Comparison

- Best straightforward heroic entry point: Beast Feast
- Best faction-and-war pressure: Five Banners Burning
- Best survival pressure: The Age of Umbra
