# Examples

## Example Rescue Fight

- Objective: prevent cultists from completing a sacrifice.
- Time pressure: ritual countdown.
- Cost of delay: each round makes the captive harder to save.
- Partial victory: captive saved, cult leader escapes.

## Example Chase Pivot

- Objective: recover a stolen relic.
- Phase change: once the thief secures the relic, the battle turns into a chase through collapsing rooftops.
