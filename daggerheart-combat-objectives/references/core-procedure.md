# Objective List

Use these as combat goals:

- Acquire or steal something important.
- Capture a target.
- Protect a person, object, or ritual.
- Escape with something.
- Break through a line or barrier.
- Delay the enemy.
- Drive someone into an ambush point.
- Destroy or activate a device.
- Force a surrender or concession.
- Survive until a countdown resolves.

## Rule

The best combat objective changes target priority, movement, or risk tolerance.

## Layering Method

Build a better fight by combining:

- one **primary objective**
- one **time pressure** such as a countdown, arrival, ritual, or escape
- one **cost of success** or partial victory condition

## Objective Questions

Ask:

- What are the adversaries trying to achieve right now?
- What would matter even if nobody died?
- What could the PCs save, stop, protect, steal, or expose?

## Partial Victory

Many strong fights should allow outcomes like:

- the PCs win the fight but lose the hostage
- the PCs save the target but the villain escapes
- the PCs stop the ritual but destroy something precious
- the PCs secure the location but expose themselves to a new threat
