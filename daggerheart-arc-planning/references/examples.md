# Examples

## Example Arc Skeleton

- Arc premise: a mercenary-backed coup is moving toward completion while one PC's family is trapped inside the city.
- A-plot: stop or expose the coup before power changes hands.
- B-plot: protect or recover the PC's family member, whose loyalties are uncertain.
- C-plot: hints that the coup's sponsor is tied to a larger neighboring power.
- Beats: infiltration, false ally, public turn, showdown at the council chamber.

## Example Ending States

- Full success: the coup fails and the sponsor is exposed.
- Partial success: the coup fails, but the sponsor escapes.
- Costly success: the city is saved, but a relationship or institution is broken.
