# Campaign Foundations

Daggerheart campaigns work best when they are personal, focused, and responsive.

## Personal

Character details are not decoration. Background answers, relationships, obligations, traumas, loyalties, and desires should become the scaffolding of later arcs.

## Focused

Prep only what the next stretch of play needs. Leave room for the story to change once players make surprising choices.

## Responsive

The world should move while the PCs act. When they chase one threat, another may grow. When they change a relationship, the setting should react.

## Map Use

Review the map between sessions:

- Which places now matter more?
- What new location follows naturally from current play?
- What blank space should stay blank for now?

## Backstory Use

When drawing on backstory, do more than name-drop it. Ask how a past relationship, mistake, or oath would create present pressure.
